99.9, The Zorg

99.9 Radio Station Broadcast

I really enjoyed creating this project because I got a chance to show a bit of creativity in a field I am not accustomed to being so creative in. While working with audition I learned a lot techniques that I think will be useful in the future for me.

The most enjoyable part of working with audition was learning how to reduce the noise in a audio clip. The process of reducing noise means pin-pointing small noises in the background, such as the AC, and eliminating those noises.
